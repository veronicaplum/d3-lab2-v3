Alternative Fuel Station and State Accessibility
Automobilies using alternative fuel types are becoming increasingly popular across the United States, yet some drivers find it difficult to locate an appropriate service station for their automobile in some areas. Here, we can examine the accessibilities of alternative fuels such as Electric(ELEC), Ethenol (E85), Liquified Natural Gas (LNG), Compressed Natural Gas (CNG), Hydrogen Gas(HY), Biodiesel (BD), and Liquified petroleum gas(LPG). This data has been distributed by 100,000 persons per state to see noticible differences. All Data is from 2013 Census and Department of Homeland Security. Map by Veronica Plum, Geography 575, Spring 2016
